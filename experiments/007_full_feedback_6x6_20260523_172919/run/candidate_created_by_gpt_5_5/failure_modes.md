# Expected Failure Modes

- Treating every missing authorization as a denial, even when a rider waives
  prior authorization.
- Applying referral requirements to PPO Gold or EPO Plus even though only HMO
  Silver has plan-level referral requirements.
- Paying out-of-network HMO/EPO claims instead of denying them.
- Forgetting that PPO Gold out-of-network allowed amount is 55 percent of the
  in-network schedule, rounded to cents by the dossier amounts.
- Applying coinsurance before deductible or copay.
- Ignoring the out-of-pocket maximum cap.
- Returning dollar strings instead of integer cents.
- Inferring from similar prior items instead of reading the current item rider
  and correspondence.
- Denying noncovered infusion only when the rider excludes it, not for all
  infusion claims.
- Missing the service-date window denial when all other evidence appears valid.
